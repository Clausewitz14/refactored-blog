Sharpening my drawing skills




From a young age, I've always had a interest in drawing. At the age of 9 - 10, I was drawing at least 10 pictures everyday. But after getting introduced to the world of coding, I was fascinated by it and completely ditched drawing. This year, I decided to reinvent the artist in me.

If you've seen my Instagram profiles, then you'll know I've uploaded a few drawings of mine. But lately, I've quit drawing and barely upload anything on my Instagram page. This year, I decided to draw at least 2 drawings a day, and upload 10 photos on my Instagram every week :D

I'll look out for interesting artists for inspiration. Email me if you know any!
