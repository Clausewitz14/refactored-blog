2021 Resolutions

With 2020 coming to an end (thank god!), I decided that it was time to take my 2021 resolutions. As a person who considers resolutions as a to-do list for one week of the new year, I decided that this year would be different. Scroll down to see all my resolutions!


Spending time outside

Cooped up with my laptop at home, I could feel very tired at times. That's why I decided to go hiking and keep myself fit this year. I decided to hike 5-8 km every month of several hiking trails in Hong Kong.
#fitnesseveryday
#hiking
#hongkonghiking



Sharpening my drawing skills

From a young age, I've always had a interest in drawing. At the age of 9 - 10, I was drawing at least 10 pictures everyday. But after getting introduced to the world of coding, I was fascinated by it and completely ditched drawing. This year, I decided to reinvent the artist in me.
#artistsoninstagram
#drawing
#2021goals



Reading a book everyday

I wanted to increase my knowledge base for 2021. Hence, I decided to read at least a part, if not more, of a book everyday. I've always been a fan of fiction and fantasy, but this year I wanted to read more history and non-fiction.
