Reading a book everyday




I wanted to increase my knowledge base for 2021. Hence, I decided to read at least a part, if not more, of a book everyday. I've always been a fan of fiction and fantasy, but this year I wanted to read more history and non-fiction.

I am a huge bookworm, but fantasy books like The Chronicles of Narnia, and fiction books are my arena. I decided to read more history and non-fiction, especially on topics like productivity, WW2 and the Holocaust.

I'm reading books on my Kindle and finding book reccomendations on Pinterest :) You can contact me at Pinterest and my Goodreads account if you have book reccomendations!
