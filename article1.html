Spending time outside




Cooped up with my laptop at home, I could feel very tired at times. That's why I decided to go hiking and keep myself fit this year. I decided to hike 5-8 km every month of several hiking trails in Hong Kong.

While I have gone hiking before, I've only gone around 5-6 kilometers. This year, I decided to hike 9-11 kilometers. Hong Kong is a great place to hike and I decided to make good use of it. I know that it will take some time and that it will not be easy, but I'm determined to stick to my goal of 1 hike per week. Follow this blog for updates!

I've already found some awesome hiking spots and am raring to go. Email me if you know some sweet spots!
